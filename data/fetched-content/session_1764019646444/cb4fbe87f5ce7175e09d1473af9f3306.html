Quantum Computing Key Concepts: Qubits, Superposition, and Entanglement

Learn essential concepts like qubits, superposition, and entanglement in "Quantum Computing Key Concepts: Qubits, Superposition, and Entanglement." Build your foundation for advanced quantum studies.

Course Overview

Overall Proficiency Level
2 - Intermediate
Course Catalog Number
T101
Course Prerequisites

None

Training Purpose
Functional Development
Management Development
Specific Audience
All
Delivery Method
Online, Self-Paced
  • Online, Self-Paced

Learning Objectives

  • Define and understand the role of qubits in quantum systems.
  • Explore the concept of superposition and its applications.
  • Examine the phenomenon of quantum entanglement.
  • Analyze how these concepts impact quantum algorithms.
  • Apply theoretical concepts to practical quantum computing challenges.
